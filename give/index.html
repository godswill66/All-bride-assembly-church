Frequently Asked Questions

Is my online giving secure?

Absolutely. We partner with industry-leading financial platforms that use the highest level of encryption and security protocols to protect your personal and financial information. We never store credit card numbers on our local servers.

Can I designate my gift?

Yes. Our online portal allows you to designate funds to specific ministries (e.g., General Fund, Missions, Building Fund) during the giving process. If mailing a check, please note the designation in the memo line.

How do I get an annual giving statement?

Annual giving statements for tax purposes are mailed or emailed automatically in late January. You can also log into your online giving account at any time to view and download your full giving history.
