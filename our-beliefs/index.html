Our Core Beliefs

The foundational truths that guide our worship, ministry, and daily lives. END TIME MESSAGE

1. The Bible

We believe the Bible (the 66 books of the Old and New Testaments) is the divinely inspired, **inerrant, and infallible Word of God**. It is the complete revelation of His will for the salvation of mankind, and it is the supreme and final authority for all Christian faith and life.

2. God (The Trinity)

We believe in one God, eternally existing in three co-equal persons: **Father, Son (Jesus Christ), and Holy Spirit**. These three are one God, having precisely the same nature, attributes, and perfection, and are worthy of precisely the same homage, confidence, and obedience.

3. Jesus Christ

We believe that Jesus Christ is fully God and fully man. He was conceived by the Holy Spirit, born of the Virgin Mary, lived a sinless life, died on the cross as the sacrificial atonement for our sins, was bodily resurrected, and ascended to the right hand of the Father where He now acts as our High Priest and Advocate.

4. Humanity & Sin

We believe that humanity was created in the image of God but fell into sin, resulting in spiritual death and eternal separation from God. All people are sinners by nature and by choice, incapable of redeeming themselves, and in desperate need of Godâ€™s saving grace.

5. Salvation

We believe that salvation is by grace alone through faith alone in Christ alone. It is a free gift of God, neither merited nor secured in whole or in part by any virtue or work of man, but received only through personal faith in the Lord Jesus Christ.

6. The Church

We believe that the Church is the body of Christ, a spiritual organism made up of all born-again believers. Its purpose is to worship God, grow in spiritual maturity, fellowship, and spread the Gospel to all nations.
