The All Bride Assembly Church

Founded in 2024 by a small group of families with a big vision, The All Bride Assembly Church was built on the belief that church should be a true family, a place where everyone is seen, known, and loved. We started in a local school LEA Primary school Dawaki Abuja Nigeria and, through faith and hard work, grew into the vibrant community you see today.

We are rooted in the gospel and committed to being a light in our city, focusing on Endtime message, bold faith, and active service.

Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To Gather people into Christ's family, to Grow them in Christ-like maturity, and to equip them to Go out and serve the world.

Our Vision

To be a place where the unchurched are welcomed, the broken are restored, and the committed are equipped to make a lasting difference in our city.
