We Can't Wait To Meet You!

Here's everything you need to know to make your first Sunday comfortable and easy.

Let Us Know You're Coming!

What to Expect on Sunday

Our goal is to be a welcoming and authentic place for everyone, regardless of where they are on their faith journey. Services last about 70 minutes and include worship, a Biblically-based message, and opportunities to connect.

Parking & Arrival

Follow the "Guest Parking" signs. Our Welcome Team will greet you at the main doors and help you find coffee and seating.

Dress Code

Come as you are! Dress responsibly. We are casual. You'll see everything from jeans and t-shirts to business casual.

Music Style

Our music is modern and passionate, led by a full band. Feel free to participate or just observe.

Kids & Students

We provide safe, fun, and age-appropriate ministry for children from infant through 5th grade during both Sunday morning services.
